Once apon a time, there was a little girl called little red riding hood.
One day, her grandmother got very ill, so her mother baked some muffins, for
little red riding hood deliver to her ill grandmother

When she started her journey, she walked into the forest and soon after, she met a wolf.
The wolf asked little red riding hood were she was going and she replied "To see my ill grandmother. She lives on a log cabin near the next hill."

As the wolf heard this, he dashed of for the next hill wanting to eat the grandmother while little red riding hood walked.

When the wolf arrived at log cabin and knocked on the door, a voice said "Who is it?", and the wolf
mimicking little red riding hoods voice said,"It's little red riding hood. Can I come in?". And suddenly, not waiting for the reply
he dashed in, eating the grandmother in one gulp and disguising as her after.

When little red riding hood finally arrived at her grandmother's house and went in, she said "what big eyes you have!"
the reply came as "better to see you with, dear" "what a big nose you have!" "better to smell you with, dear" "what a big mouth you have!"
"better to eat you with!", closely followed by a terrifying roar as the wolf jumped up, threatening to eat little red riding hood.
Luckily, a hunter heard the roar and came. He killed the wolf and the grandmother climbed out and The hunter, little red riding hood, and her grandmother lived happily ever after.
